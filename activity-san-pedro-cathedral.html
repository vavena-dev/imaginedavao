Top Tripadvisor Pick

San Pedro Cathedral

Why It Ranks High

San Pedro Cathedral, one of Davao's oldest churches, is a recurring heritage stop on city itineraries and review rankings. The site offers a quieter contrast to markets and malls, with architectural details and local religious history in the city center.

  • Historic Catholic landmark in central Davao
  • Architecture-focused stop for heritage walking tours
  • Calm reflective environment in a busy district
  • Easy to pair with nearby museums and parks
