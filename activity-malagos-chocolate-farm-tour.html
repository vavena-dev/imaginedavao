Malagos Chocolate & Farm Tour

What to Expect

This farm-to-table tour takes guests through cacao cultivation, fermentation and artisan chocolate crafting. Visitors can join tasting flights, meet local makers and pair chocolates with fresh Davao fruits and coffee.

  • Guided farm and production walkthrough.
  • Chocolate tasting session with flavor notes.
  • Souvenir stop with award-winning local products.
  • Optional lunch at an on-site garden restaurant.

Best For

Food lovers, couples and small groups looking for a slow, flavorful Davao day trip.

Start between 10:00 AM and 11:00 AM to align with the best tasting windows.
