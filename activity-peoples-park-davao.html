Top Tripadvisor Pick

People's Park

Why It Ranks High

People's Park offers a central green break between Davao's commercial districts and remains a top-ranked stop for first-time visitors. The park's pathways, open lawns and distinctive sculptures make it an easy walking destination for families and casual city sightseeing.

  • Landscaped park with public art and shaded areas
  • Photogenic city landmark in a central location
  • Family-friendly pace and accessible walking paths
  • Good downtime stop between museum and market visits
