Heritage Dining

Tiny Kitchen & Dulce Vida

What to Expect

This chef-led dining room is known for set menus that reinterpret Mindanao staples with modern plating. Expect a relaxed pace, attentive service, and a progression that highlights local produce and regional sauces.

  • Seasonal tasting route focused on Davao ingredients.
  • Optional wine and non-alcoholic pairing program.
  • Ideal for date nights and small private celebrations.
  • Advance reservations strongly recommended on weekends.

Best For

Couples, food-focused travelers, and visitors seeking a premium local tasting journey.

Planning Tip

Reserve dinner between 6:00 PM and 7:30 PM, then pair it with a short Poblacion night walk.
