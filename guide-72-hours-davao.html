First Timers

72 Hours in Davao

A no-stress three-day plan that combines city culture, highland air and a coastal final day so first-time visitors can experience Davao with confidence.

Suggested Route

  • Day 1: Poblacion orientation walk, museums, riverside dinner and evening café circuit.
  • Day 2: Early transfer to upland farms, chocolate making session and sunset viewpoint.
  • Day 3: Samal crossing, beach stop sequence and Gulf sunset return dinner.

Trip Snapshot

  • Best for: First-time visitors
  • Pace: Balanced with flexible breaks
  • Transport: Mix of private transfer and short ferry
  • Ideal season: Dry months and Kadayawan window
