Kadayawan Culture Loop

Trace Davao's living heritage through galleries, weaving communities and festival touchpoints with a route designed for meaningful cultural immersion.

Suggested Route

  • Morning: City museum orientation and curated history walk in the old district core.
  • Midday: Visit an artisan studio with live weaving and beadwork demonstrations.
  • Evening: Festival rehearsal grounds, live music stop and regional tasting dinner.
