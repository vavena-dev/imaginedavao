Downtown Davao | August Program

Kadayawan Grand Parade

The Kadayawan Grand Parade is a high-energy celebration of Davao's cultural pride, with floral floats, dance contingents, and city-wide performances. It is the signature event for travelers who want the full festival atmosphere.

Back to Events Book

Why Join

  • Largest annual culture-forward event in Davao
  • Strong visual route with floral and costume showcases
  • Excellent for first-time city visitors

Best Timing

  • Morning arrival for route-side seating options
  • Afternoon for full parade momentum
  • Evening for post-event dining around downtown

Suggested Pairing

  1. Lunch in Poblacion after the main parade pass
  2. Sunset photo stop along city viewing points
  3. Night market walk to complete your festival day
