Top Tripadvisor Pick

Matina Town Square

Why It Ranks High

Matina Town Square is a long-standing social hub in Davao where travelers can sample nightlife without moving across multiple neighborhoods. The area combines music venues, cafés and laid-back bars, making it a practical one-stop evening destination.

  • Multiple dining and nightlife options in one complex
  • Live music and social atmosphere after dark
  • Easy add-on after downtown tours
  • Suitable for casual group nights out
